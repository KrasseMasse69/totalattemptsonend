# TotalAttemptsOnEnd

A Geometry Dash mod that shows **total** level stats (attempts, playtime, jumps) on the level complete screen instead of session-only stats.

<img src="./logo.png" alt="TotalAttemptsOnEnd logo" />

## Features
**TotalAttemptsOnEnd** is a Geometry Dash mod that shows your **lifetime** level stats on the "Level Complete!" screen. Instead of only seeing session attempts, playtime, and jumps, this mod replaces those numbers with the total amount you've accumulated across all attempts.

- **Total Attempts** – See how many times you've *really* attempted a level
- **Total Playtime** – Lifetime playtime instead of just the run's time
- **Fully Configurable** – Toggle each stat on/off in Geode's mod settings
  
## Why?

Ever looked at the "Level Complete!" screen and thought *"Wait, that's only from this session... I've definitely died way more times than that."* Yeah, me too. This mod just shows the real numbers — your total attempts, total playtime, and total jumps for the level.

## How do I use it?

Just install it and play! The stats on the level complete screen will automatically show your lifetime totals.  

You can toggle each stat on or off in the mod settings:

1. Open **Geode → Mods → TotalAttemptsOnEnd**
2. Check or uncheck any of the three options:
   - **Show Total Attempts**
   - **Show Total Playtime**

## Usage

After completing a level, the stats on the "Level Complete!" screen will show your lifetime totals.  
Open **Geode → Mods → TotalAttemptsOnEnd** to toggle each stat on/off.
